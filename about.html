About Me

Hi, I am Thomas Procee and am currently studying at Univeristy College Utrecht. I am majoring in Earth and Environment and Molecular Cell Biology.

This website serves to illustrate the series of maps I have created for my Geographical Information Systems (GIS) lab. In this lab I created various maps and analyzed them using software like QGIS, ArcGIS, and Google Earth Engine. This lab has allowed me to make different maps with different purposes which illustrates how maps can be used in many fields and demonstrates why these tools and this lab is useful for me.
