Vector Map

This vector map is to show the amount of trees in the various neighborhoods of Utrecht. Trees are important for the happiness and the mental health of the residents and so I wanted to highlight which neighborhoods had many trees and which ones less. Obviously density matters as some neighborhoods are larger than others but I think it is still interesting to highlight how in some neighborhoods there are around 36,000 trees planted.
